Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information

Last Updated: May 2025

Important Notice: This privacy policy explains how MotoWeb collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Types of Information We Collect

To provide you with the best possible service, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password
  • Personal Profile: Biography, avatar, location, interests
  • Vehicle Information: Motorcycle model, brand, year, modifications
  • Activity Data: Posts, photos, comments, likes, shares
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, visit timestamps

1.2 Collection Methods

  • Direct user input (registration, posting, photo uploads)
  • Automated collection during website usage
  • Cookie and similar technology collection for browsing behavior
  • Third-party services (such as social login providers)

2. Information Usage

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Creating and managing your account, authentication
  • Content Display: Showing your posts, works, and social interactions
  • Personalization: Recommending relevant content and customizing interface
  • Communication: Sending important notifications and responding to inquiries
  • Security: Preventing fraud and abuse
  • Analysis: Understanding usage patterns to improve platform features
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

3.1 Public Information

The following information may be publicly displayed based on your privacy settings:

  • Username, avatar, and personal bio
  • Modified vehicle works and photos
  • Community posts, comments, and likes
  • Vehicle information (if chosen to be public)

3.2 Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Who help us operate the platform
  • Legal Requirements: In response to court orders or government investigations
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset transfer
  • Emergency Situations: To protect user safety or prevent crime

4. Data Protection

We implement various measures to protect your personal information:

  • Technical Protection: SSL encryption, secure data transmission, server security
  • Access Control: Limited employee access and strict authorization procedures
  • Monitoring System: 24/7 security monitoring and anomaly detection
  • Regular Updates: System security updates and vulnerability patches
  • Employee Training: Data protection training and confidentiality agreements

5. Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request to view your personal information we hold
  • Right to Rectification: Request to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request to delete your personal information
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request to limit the use of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request to receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to our use of your personal information

6. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Detect security threats and prevent abuse

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though disabling cookies may affect website functionality. For more information, please read our Cookie Policy.

7. Minor Protection

We take special care to protect minors' privacy:

  • Children under 13 are not allowed to register or use our services
  • Users aged 13-18 require parental or guardian consent
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • If such collection is discovered, we will promptly delete the information

8. International Data Transfer

Your personal information may be transferred to countries outside Taiwan. In such cases, we ensure:

  • Appropriate safeguards for international organizations
  • Standard contractual clauses to ensure data protection standards
  • Regular review of transfer necessity and security

9. Policy Updates

We may periodically update this privacy policy. For significant changes:

  • We will display notices on the website
  • Notify registered users via email
  • Provide 30 days notice before changes
  • Show revision date on updated pages
