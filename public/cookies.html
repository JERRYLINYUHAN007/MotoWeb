Cookie Policy

Learn how we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience

Last Updated: May 2025

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a better user experience.

1Purpose of Cookies

MotoWeb uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

Essential Functions Ensure website operation and remember your login status
Personalization Remember your preferences and language settings
Performance Monitor website usage and improve speed
Security Prevent fraud and abuse
Analytics Understand user behavior and improve services
Content Recommendations Suggest relevant modification content and parts

2Types of Cookies

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

Session Management: Maintain your login status
Security Authentication: Prevent cross-site request forgery
Shopping Cart: Remember selected items
Form Data: Prevent duplicate form submissions

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your personal experience:

Preferences: Remember language and theme settings
Location: Provide location-based content
Social Features: Remember social interaction settings

2.3 Performance Cookies

These cookies help us improve website performance:

Analytics: Track page views and user behavior
Load Times: Monitor and optimize loading speed
Error Tracking: Identify and fix technical issues

2.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies help us deliver relevant content:

Recommendations: Show personalized modification suggestions
Advertising: Display relevant motorcycle parts and services
Social Media: Enable sharing and social features

3Cookie Management

3.1 Browser Settings

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
Edge: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies

3.2 Cookie Preferences

You can customize your cookie preferences on our website:

Essential cookies cannot be disabled
Functional cookies can be managed in your account settings
Performance cookies can be opted out
Marketing cookies require explicit consent
